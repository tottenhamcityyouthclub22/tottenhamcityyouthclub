About us

Who We Are

Tottenham City Youth Club is a Community Youth Based Organization situated at Mamobi-West Accra-GHANA, established in 2015.

On 3rd October 2017, the club was certified with the certificate of Incorporation and to commence business as a Non-Governmental Organization by the Registrar General Department Republic of Ghana.

  • The main objectives of the club are to promote and provide for the moral, educational, physical and mental wellbeing of members and the community entirely.
  • We are aimed at assisting Youth acquire the necessary life skills for their own benefit and that of their communities, Ghana and the world at large.
  • Our aim is to use education as a tool to reduce social vices and crime like drugs and alcohol abuse, robbery and internet fraud by spotting brilliant but needy students and support them in any way we can to help them achieve their educational goal.

Our Mission

To combat zero hunger and poverty, offer additional methods and education to the less privileged members of society through sports activities that will actually create businesses and jobs.

Our Vision

We envision to build sports facilities with a hub for education in deprived communities in all parts of the world.

Our Core Values

Discipline, Love, Enthusiastic, Passion for a Better Society

Let's do this together

We welcome funds, pledges, and other material forms of sponsorship from donors to help us meet the educational, health and developmental needs of our beneficiaries.
